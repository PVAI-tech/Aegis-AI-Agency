The Brief

Client Requirements

Slay Beauty London needed a premium storefront to sell curated haircare, skincare and body essentials from 50+ trusted brands — with clear delivery promises, seasonal campaign pages, and a way to help customers navigate 500+ products without feeling overwhelmed. The standout ask was an AI "Beauty Concierge" that could recommend the right product based on a customer's hair type, skin concern or budget, right on the site.

Challenge

With 500+ products across dozens of brands, customers were struggling to find the right product for their specific hair type, skin concern or budget — generic filters weren't enough to feel personal, and the brand risked losing sales to decision fatigue.

Solution

We designed and built the Beauty Concierge — a conversational AI assistant embedded directly on the site that recommends products in plain English based on hair type, skin concern or budget. It sits alongside a redesigned storefront with a trust-building hero, clear delivery messaging, seasonal campaign banners and a fast, mobile-first product grid.
