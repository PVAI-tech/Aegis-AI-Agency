The Brief

What This Demo Showcases

Atlantic Pearl is a self-initiated concept build for a fictional Ghana beachfront resort — designed to show hospitality clients exactly what's possible: immersive full-bleed photography that sells the experience before a guest reads a word, a clear rooms and rates page, an amenities showcase, a booking enquiry flow ready to connect to email, Google Sheets, CRM or WhatsApp automation, and an optional 24/7 AI guest concierge layered on top.

Challenge

Hotels lose bookings after hours — a guest browsing at 11pm with a question about check-in times or airport transfers has no one to ask, and a generic contact form often isn't enough to convert a hesitant browser into a confirmed booking.

Solution

Alongside the core booking enquiry flow, we designed an optional 24/7 AI Guest Concierge that answers FAQs, recommends rooms, quotes on things like airport transfers, and captures WhatsApp leads instantly — so the hotel never misses an enquiry, even while staff are asleep.

Status
Concept Demo

This is a self-initiated demo, not a live client site — built to show hospitality brands exactly what a premium booking site with an AI concierge can look like. Pricing depends on your rooms, brand assets and how deep the AI concierge integration goes.
