# SOP — Discovery

Part of [`AegisOperatingSystem.md`](../AegisOperatingSystem.md). Covers stages 3–5 of [`ClientJourney.md`](../ClientJourney.md) (Discovery call → Research), the bridge between "signed a contract" and "know enough to build."

## Who does this today
Laiba Sheikh, solo.

## Trigger
A discovery call is booked (via Calendly, or immediately following a qualified Sales conversation).

## Process
1. **Run the discovery call** with two goals: understand the business's actual goals/pain points, and openly discuss which of the three motions (Productised AI / Custom Development / Managed Services — see [`BusinessModel.md`](../BusinessModel.md)) actually fits. Don't let the client self-select the wrong motion out of unfamiliarity with the difference.
2. **Sanity-check budget and timeline live**, on the call — not left for a proposal to reveal a mismatch later.
3. **Send the Discovery Questionnaire** ([`templates/DiscoveryQuestionnaire.md`](../templates/DiscoveryQuestionnaire.md)) within 24 hours of the call. This captures what a live conversation misses: brand assets, existing tool/login access needed, content readiness.
4. **Chase once** if the questionnaire isn't returned within a week. Don't chase repeatedly — treat non-response as a signal to requalify the lead's seriousness.
5. **Research phase**: review competitor/reference sites the client mentions, and confirm the technical feasibility of any requested integration *before* it gets quoted — a feature promised in a proposal that turns out to be technically infeasible is a much worse conversation than a short delay here.

## What "discovery complete" looks like
A returned questionnaire, a feasibility-checked feature list, and enough clarity to write a specific (not range-based) proposal.

## Common failure mode to avoid
Skipping the feasibility check and quoting a feature the team hasn't actually verified is buildable in the timeframe promised — this is exactly the "capability gap" risk flagged in [`BusinessModel.md`](../BusinessModel.md) for categories like mobile apps or large enterprise platforms.

## Handoff notes for a future hire
This role needs access to `templates/DiscoveryQuestionnaire.md` and enough technical fluency (or a direct line to whoever has it) to sanity-check feasibility claims before they reach a client.
