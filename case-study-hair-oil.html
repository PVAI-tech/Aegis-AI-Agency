The Brief

Client Requirements

H.Sheikh London needed a debut site for their first product — a botanical "Hair Elixir" — that felt like a luxury skincare launch rather than a typical beauty e-commerce store. That meant editorial-grade photography, real ingredient storytelling (Argan, Avocado and Sunflower oil), social proof, and a way to reassure first-time visitors on a single-product site that this was the right ritual for their hair.

The AI Assistant

Meet Hera

Every H.Sheikh visitor gets their own personal hair ritual concierge.

Hera — Your Personal Hair Ritual Assistant

On a single-product site, the biggest risk is a visitor leaving unsure if the elixir is right for them. Hera sits in the corner of every page as a warm, approachable chat concierge — not a corporate chatbot — ready to ask about someone's hair type and recommend the right ritual instantly.

"Hi, I'm Hera ✨ How can I help you today?"
Challenge

With just one hero product rather than a big catalogue, the entire site has to do the selling through trust and storytelling alone. Generic FAQ pages don't build the same confidence as talking to someone — and without live customer support, undecided first-time visitors were likely to leave without buying.

Solution

We paired the editorial storytelling — ingredients, benefits, brand origin — with Hera, a friendly AI concierge embedded on every page. Visitors can ask about their hair type or concern and get an instant, personal recommendation instead of bouncing to compare elsewhere.

Pre-Launch
